Child Safety Standards Policy — Vivah Sagar

Last updated: August 21, 2025

Vivah Sagar is committed to maintaining a safe and trusted environment in full compliance with Google Play’s Child Safety Standards and all applicable laws.

1) Scope & Audience

  • Vivah Sagar is strictly intended for users aged 18 years and above.
  • The platform is a matrimonial service and is not directed toward children.
  • We do not knowingly collect or store personal data from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us at app.indiawebsoft@gmail.com so we can take appropriate action.

2) Zero-Tolerance for CSAM

Vivah Sagar follows a strict zero-tolerance policy for any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or content involving the exploitation, endangerment, or sexualization of minors. This includes:

  • Images or videos of minors in sexual or inappropriate contexts
  • Text, messages, or profiles that encourage exploitation or grooming
  • Links or references to CSAM or illegal content

Any such content is immediately removed and reported to relevant authorities as required by law.

3) User-Generated Content & Moderation

  • Prohibited Content:
    • CSAM or any sexualized depictions of minors
    • Grooming, solicitation, or attempts to contact minors
    • Harassment or threats involving minors
    • Fake profiles pretending to be minors
  • Moderation & Enforcement:
    • Immediate removal of violating content
    • Suspension or termination of user accounts
    • Device or IP blocking in severe cases
    • Reporting to legal authorities when necessary
  • Both automated tools and human moderators review reports and suspicious activity.

4) Reporting Child-Safety Concerns

If you notice any content or behavior that may endanger a child:

  • Email us at: app.indiawebsoft@gmail.com
  • Use the in-app “Report” or “Contact Support” option (if available)
  • Please include screenshots, profile links, or timestamps to help us respond quickly.

5) Cooperation with Authorities

Vivah Sagar complies with applicable laws regarding the reporting of child safety threats. When required, we will preserve relevant information and share it with authorized agencies.

6) Data Handling Practices

We do not knowingly collect or process personal information belonging to minors. If we discover that a user under 18 has registered or their data is present, we will:

  • Delete the account
  • Remove associated data
  • Prevent future access where necessary

7) Violations & Consequences

Any violation of this child safety policy may result in content removal, account restriction, temporary suspension, or permanent termination—without prior notice.
