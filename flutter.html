  • Hourly & Daily Forecast API: Provides hourly and 7-day forecasts including precipitation probability and expected rainfall.
  • Rain Alert API: Sends notifications when rain is predicted in a user-selected location (morning/evening reminders).
  • Multiple Location API: Allows users to add and track weather for multiple cities or locations.
  • User Preferences API: Stores notification preferences, subscription settings, and selected locations for personalized alerts.

OpenWeather API — Quick Summary

  • Current Weather Data: Get live weather conditions for any location, including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and weather description.
  • Hourly & Daily Forecasts: Retrieve detailed hourly and 7-day forecasts, including precipitation probability and expected rainfall.
  • Weather Alerts: Access weather warnings and alerts for extreme conditions like storms, heavy rain, or snow.
  • Geocoding API: Convert city names to geographic coordinates and vice versa for accurate weather retrieval.
  • Multiple Locations Support: Fetch weather data for multiple cities or coordinates simultaneously.
  • Integration: Easy to integrate with mobile or web apps to provide real-time weather updates and notifications.
